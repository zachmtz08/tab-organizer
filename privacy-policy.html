Tab Organizer – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

What data does this extension access?

Tab Organizer reads the title and URL of your open browser tabs in order to display and group them by task category.

Is any data collected or sent anywhere?

No. Tab Organizer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data. All processing happens locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

Does this extension use cookies or tracking?

No. There are no cookies, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

Third-party services

This extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
