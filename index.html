Tab Organizer

Chrome extension for taming your tabs.

Auto-group tabs by task, save named sessions, surface tabs you've forgotten, and write your own grouping rules — with optional pixel buddies on every page.

What it does

  • Smart auto-grouping into 14 built-in categories (plus your own).
  • Named sessions you can save and restore later.
  • Stale-tab badge and one-click Save & Close.
  • Custom rules — paste a URL or type a domain, pick a category.
  • Search, close duplicates, dark mode, pixel buddies.
